SAN FRANCISCO BOOTBLACK

Save the Date!

March 2-3, 2018
Leather Alliance Weekend

The San Francisco Bay Area Leather Alliance is excited to announce the addition of a new title to the Leather Alliance Weekend in 2018. The first San Francisco Bootblack title will be held along side the 36th Mr San Francisco Leather contest. The bootblack contest is open to all, regardless of identified gender and the titleholder will represent and help grow Bootblack community in the greater San Francisco bay area.
